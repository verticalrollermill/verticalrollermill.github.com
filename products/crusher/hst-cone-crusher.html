Working principle

Machine of HST series single cylinder hydraulic cone crusher, the motor rotates to drive the transmission shaft with device through a belt pulley and a triangular, the drive shaft drives the eccentric sleeve to rotate through the size of bevel gear, the moving cone through the main shaft in the eccentric sleeve do swing movement under the action of the dynamic and static cone, cone and close, sometimes far away from the rolling mortar wall, broken cavity material between rolling mortar wall and the crushing machine is continuously pressed, the impact crushing, crushing material from the lower part of the discharge.
