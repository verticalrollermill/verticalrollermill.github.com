XSD Series Sand Washing Machine

XSD series sand washing machine, commonly known as bucket wheel sand washing machine, the Shanghai World state machine uses advanced technology, combined with the actual situation of the domestic industry to develop efficient and gravel sand washing equipment with Sand production use.

Detailed introduction

  • Product advantage
  • Technical data
  • Main Components
  • 1. Processing a large quantity of low production cost
    Single output up to 180 tons / hour, can basically meet the needs of customers of the sand washing machine; less investment, low power, low power consumption, effectively save the cost of production.
  • 2. The multiple effects of high cleanliness
    The device with cleaning, dewatering, grade three functions; when working, the impeller drive, sand grinding each other, remove the gravel covered surface impurities, and destruction of water vapor coated layer of sand in the strong currents, and complete the cleaning and impurity removing function.
  • 3. Simple structure low fault rate
    The sand washing machine has the advantages of simple structure, reasonable structure, impeller transmission bearing device with water and under water material isolation, novel seal structure, fully enclosed oil bath type transmission device, to avoid much of the bearings for soaking, sand and pollutants caused damaged phenomenon.
  • 4. Long service life and no pollution
    Design of sealing structure layout, good effective, thus the machine can long-term need repair, durable; low water consumption, low working noise, in line with national environmental standards.

Working principle

When working, the motor through a triangle belt, a speed reducer, gear reducer drives the impeller to rotate slowly, sand has a feed chute into the washing groove, tumbling in the impeller drive, and mutual grinding, remove the gravel covered surface impurities, and destruction of water vapor coated layer of sand, in order to facilitate water dehydration; at the same time, the formation of a strong water flow, in a timely manner to the small proportion of the impurities and foreign away, and from the overflow outlet washing tank discharge, complete cleaning effect. Clean gravel from the leaves away, the last gravel into the discharge chute from the rotation of the impeller, complete sand cleaning effect.
