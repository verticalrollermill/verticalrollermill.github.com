GX High Efficiency Thickener

I produced the GX series of highly efficient enrichment machine structure and the center drive thickener similar to its main feature is the addition of a certain amount of flocculant to be concentrated in the pulp, so that the pulp floc formation of mineral particles to accelerate their settling velocity and then concentrated to improve efficiency. GX-efficiency concentrator, including the host, and add flocculant preparation, automatic control and other components.

  • Advantages
  • 1. adding flocculants increased sedimentation of solid particles of diameter, thereby accelerating the sedimentation rate;
  • 2. the installation of mineral particles inclined plate sedimentation distance shortened, increasing the settlement area;
  • 3. play a mud layer of dense phase deposition flocculation, filtration, compression, and enhance the role of the amount of processing;
  • 4. is equipped with a complete self-control facilities.
