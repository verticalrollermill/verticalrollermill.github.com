XCF/KYF Series Flotation Machine

XCF-type flotation machine with KYF belong inflatable flotation machine, which are widely used non-ferrous metal, black metal and non-metallic minerals beneficiation operations. Often both supporting the use of both structural features similar to the same dimensions.

  • Product advantage

  • 3. The impeller works as a centrifugal pump, so as to make the solid particles suspending.
