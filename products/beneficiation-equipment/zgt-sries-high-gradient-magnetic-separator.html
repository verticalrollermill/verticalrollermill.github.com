ZGT Series High Gradient Magnetic Separator

Our group can provide ZGT electro high gradient magnetic separator, the use of magnetic force, pulsating fluid force and gravity force field of comprehensive beneficiation production. Is mainly suitable for fine-grained (less than 0.074mm (-200 mesh) for 60-100% or 2mm below) of the hematite ore, manganese ore, ilmenite, wolframite, tantalum niobium ore and other weak magnetic minerals: quartz, feldspar and the dressing, nepheline, fluorite, sillimanite, Li Huishi, kaolin and other non-metal ore iron removal, purification.

  • 1. Their separation dimension variety is significant and also the max separation size is2.0mm. Consequently, it simplifies classification get the job done at working internet sites.
  • 2. Slurry pulsating units keep ore particles loose all of the time, as a result improving quality of magnetic concentrates.
  • 3. The ring is rotated vertically and concentrates are washed reversely, so magnetic medium is not going to be blocked quickly.
  • 4. High beneficiation ratio and large recovery charge; Very simple operation and easy maintenance with great adaptability.
