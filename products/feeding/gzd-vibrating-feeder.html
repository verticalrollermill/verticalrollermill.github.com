GZD Series Vibrating Feeder

Vibrating feeder is also called vibration feeding machine, in the production process for the massive, granular and powdery materials from the storage warehouse or funnel uniform for the quantitative or the feed to feeding equipment. In the crushing production line, the product can be as crushing machinery continuous and uniform feeding, and the initial screening material.

Detailed introduction

  • Technical data
  • Main Components
  • 1. High efficiency and low operating cost
    The double eccentric shaft as the excitation source, amplitude stability, guarantee the liquidity of vibrating ore rock material, reducing channel blocking phenomenon of red material, so the ore drawing capacity, high efficiency; Has the advantages of simple structure, no moving parts, less wearing parts, the series of vibration and less power consumption, obvious energy saving.
  • 2. High adaptability of less investment
    On various kinds of ore and rock in particular cohesive ore rock material adaptability, and can work in dusty or water more environment; Small volume, light weight, simple and compact structure, less investment, high performance price ratio.
  • 3. Stable and reliable working performance
    Good continuity of the machine, the exciting force can be adjusted; Electrical control using half wave rectification electric appliance, stepless regulation of feeding materials, can be used for automatic control of the production process, the realization of the production process automation.
  • 4. Low noise and no pollution
    The machine has low noise, closed frame structure can be, to prevent dust pollution.

Working principle

Feeder is made to the trough body, exciter, spring bearing, gears and other components. Vibration source tank feeding the exciter vibration exciter is composed of two eccentric shafts (active and passive) and gear pair composed by motor through V-belt drive axle, and then by the drive shaft gear driven shaft rotation, active and passive reverse rotation axes simultaneously, so that the tank vibration, continuous flow of materials to achieve the purpose of transporting materials.
