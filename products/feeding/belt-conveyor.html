Belt Conveyor

Shanghai Zenith Belt Conveyor is the company with the actual situation in mining, coal, metallurgy and other industries production line, developed high-performance belt conveyor equipment. The conveyor modular component design, which greatly simplify the production process, reduce the difficulty of installation and removal, logistics cost savings, but also can shorten the stocking cycle, improve product quality, ease of ongoing maintenance.

  • 1. Fast and reliable module design
    The main beam and the supporting legs adopt modularization design, simplify the production process, shorten the delivery cycle; between each module assembly are connected by a bolt, reliable connection, convenient assembly; packaging and reliability, greatly reducing the damage of equipment during transport, security equipment arrived at the scene can be quickly assembled and put into use.
  • 2. The optimization of the structure is firm and practical
    Equipment structure optimization, beam profiles using C steel, compared with the same channel, the weight is light, but increased, the flexural performance of firmer; leg changed to Y type support leg, support more firmly, stacking space is greatly increased and the.
  • 3. Optimization of transmission efficient and convenient
    Equipment with exterior type high-efficiency electric drum, and optimize the installation form, compared with the transmission mode, common external motor reducer more light; and a built-in electric drum compared, better heat dissipation performance. For the electric roller high power, to improve the startup performance adopts hydraulic coupler; according to the need of braking working environment, can also be provided with the brake; if the explosion-proof safety requirements, can be the first to use flameproof motor; electric drum also can choose a full magnetic or semi magnetic, used in mineral processing, iron removal conditions.
  • 4. A variety of configuration feature rich
    The equipment configuration of the head and tail of the cleaning device, and a material guiding groove, can also according to user requirements allocation repair platform, protective cover, a hopper and a backstop function components. In addition, users can also according to the use requirements customized special belt conveyor, such as mobile belt conveyors etc.

Working principle

Belt conveyor is a kind of friction drive, in a continuous manner for material transport machinery, the machine is composed of a driving device, tension device, belt the middle frame and the roller component, it uses the conveyor belt as traction and load-bearing components, and to the continuous conveying of bulk materials or goods into pieces, and then the material / object feed point from the initial continuous conveying to the final point of unloading. The machine can be used not only in the delivery of granular materials, can also be carried into the delivery of items.
