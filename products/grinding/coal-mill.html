Coal Mill

Coal mill is the coal into a coal crushing and mechanical, it is an important auxiliary equipment in pulverized coal boiler. Coal mill process is the process of coal was broken and its surface area increased. To add more surface area, must overcome the binding force between molecules and solids, energy consumption. The coal is ground into pulverized coal in the coal grinding machine, mainly is carries on through the crush, crush and grind in three ways. The crushing process energy consumption of the province. Trituration process most expend energy. Various mill are both in the milling process of the above two or three way, but in what is considered the main types of coal mill and.

Coal grinding machine is composed of feeding part, discharging part, a rotary part, a transmission part (reducer, small gear, motors, electrical control) and other major parts. Hollow shaft adopts steel castings, the liner is detachable, slewing gear hobbing with casting, the cylinder body with wear-resistant liner, with good resistance to wear. This machine is stable in operation, reliable work.

Working principle

This machine is a horizontal cylinder rotating device, outer gear, two warehouses, lattice type coal mill. Material from the feed devicefeedair evenly into the mill screw shaft first warehouse, the warehouse has stepped lining board or corrugated liner, built in different specifications of steel balls, rotating cylinder pushes the ball to a certain height to generate centrifugal force after the fall, the material produces severe impact and abrasive. Materials in the first position to coarse grinding, the board by the single compartment into the second warehouse, the warehouse linedwith flat lining, steel ball inside, the material to further grinding. Powder through the discharge grate discharge, finish grinding operation.
